Your day, one calm timeline.

A focus and day planner built for minds that lose track of time. Tasks and calendar events live on one timeline, high-priority alerts follow up instead of ringing once and giving up, and everything syncs privately through your own iCloud. No account, no server of ours.

Features

  • Today view. Tasks and calendar events on one timeline.
  • Time-blocking. Creates real events on your calendar.
  • Focus timer. Automatic breaks between sessions.
  • Quick capture. Understands dates and recurrence as you type.
  • Projects, tags, subtasks. Plus photo attachments.
  • Escalating alerts. High-priority tasks get a follow-up nudge if the first one is missed.
  • Location reminders. For things tied to a place.
  • Live Activity. A countdown for what's due next.
  • Widgets. Complete a task from the home screen without opening the app.
  • Apple Watch. A companion for today's list.
  • Share sheet. Add tasks from Safari, Photos, and other apps.
