Tide

A breathing timer that stays out of your way.

Pick a pattern, follow the rhythm, and let everything else settle. Your sessions are saved privately on your device — so the only thing on your mind is your breath.

Features

  • Three breathing patterns
  • Visual and haptic breath guide
  • Flexible session duration
  • Session history
  • Dark mode throughout
  • No accounts. No tracking. No noise.

Patterns

Box breathing
inhale 4 · hold 4 · exhale 4 · hold 4
Equal parts in, hold, out, hold. Used to reset under pressure and restore calm focus.
4-7-8
inhale 4 · hold 7 · exhale 8
A long exhale that signals to your nervous system it's safe to slow down.
Calm
inhale 3 · hold 2 · exhale 5 · hold 2
A gentle everyday pattern for quiet moments and transitions.
