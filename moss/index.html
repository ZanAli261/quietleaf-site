Moss

A meditation timer that stays out of your way.

Pick a duration, choose an ambient sound or silence, and breathe. Your streaks, session history, and mindful minutes are saved privately on your device — so the only thing on your mind is your practice.

Features

  • Session timer with flexible durations
  • Ambient sounds or silence
  • Interval chimes and haptics
  • Session history and streaks
  • Apple Health integration
  • No accounts. No tracking. No noise.

Support & privacy

Questions, feedback, or bug reports — email support@quietleaf.dev. We read everything.

Moss reports "Data Not Collected" on its App Store privacy label. There is no server collecting anything.
