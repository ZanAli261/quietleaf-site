How we build

No accounts. No tracking. Every Quiet Leaf app reports "Data Not Collected" on its App Store privacy label, because there is no server collecting anything.

No guilt. No streak-shaming, no red badges, no notifications designed to pull you back in. These are tools you pick up when you need them and put down when you don't.

Offline first. Your data lives on your device. Where sync exists, it runs through your own private iCloud — we never see it.
